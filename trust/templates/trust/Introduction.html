You have been randomly and anonymously paired with a participant in the other room. For this session, you've been chosen to be the Investor.
Investors will start with $10 while the trustee will start with $0.


1. Investors will send a certain amount of money to the trustee, which will be tripled before being sent.
2. Trustees will have the choice to return any of the tripled amount back to the investor.
3. Each round restarts your earnings.
4. Although you'll be given $10 to start each round, the amount you earn from each round will accumulate separately.
5. Total earnings will determine how many raffle tickets you win for the lottery.


Please take the separate Qualtrics survey in the other window between the trials of the game. If you have any questions, a research assistant will be outside to help you. Good luck!
